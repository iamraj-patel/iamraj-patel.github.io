PROTOCOL MANUAL

This manual decodes the terminology used in the "Endgame Protocol" portfolio.

1. THE TIME HEIST

Standard Resume Term: Professional Summary / About Me
Just as the Avengers traveled through time to gather the stones, this section summarizes my journey through different technologies and roles to become the Cloud Engineer I am today.

2. INFINITY STONES

Standard Resume Term: Technical Skills
Each "Stone" represents a core pillar of my technical expertise:
  • Space Stone (Blue): Cloud Computing (Azure, AWS) - The ability to work anywhere.
  • Reality Stone (Red): Virtualization (VMware) - Creating virtual environments.
  • Time Stone (Green): Automation (PowerShell) - Saving time through scripts.
  • Power Stone (Purple): Systems Administration - The raw power of infrastructure management.

3. ENDGAME SCENARIOS

Standard Resume Term: Projects
These are the "battles" I've won. Specific implementations of code and infrastructure that solve real-world problems.

4. TIMELINE

Standard Resume Term: Work Experience
A chronological record of my professional history, detailing the roles I've held and the companies I've served.

5. ASSEMBLE

Standard Resume Term: Contact
The call to action. How to reach me for recruitment or collaboration.
